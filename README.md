# Axia-Marketing-campaign-
The project used  Microsoft Power BI to create  an interactive dashboard and investigates the performance of a digital marketing campaign  carried out from November 2024 to February 2025 for a company based in India across five(5) channels; Google ads, Email Campaigns, Instagram ads, Influencer marketing and Referrals for categories of products ranging from household items, benverages, Groceries, Personal Care to Snacks
A total of Two Hundred and Forty -Two Million (242M) Impressions were launched attracting Twelve Million (12M) clicks and One Million (IM) conversions. through One Thousand (1000) campaigns  A total of Three Hundred and Forty-Four Million, Three Hundred and Thirty Thousand Indian Rupees (344,33M INR) in revenue was generated from sales. The sum of Two Million, Four Hundred and Twenty Thousand Indian Rupee (2.42M INR) was spent on all the marketing platforms. A otal of Three Hundred and Fifty- Nine Thousand, Nine Hundred and Twenty (359.92K) was realized as return on Investment (ROI) within the period.
Highest Revenue by product was Cold drinks and lowest Energy drink, Highest Revenue by category was Household and the least was Personal Care, Google Ads generated the most revenue and Emails the lowest.
Household items and Beverage had the highest and lowest Return on Investments, Air freshener and Cofee for products respectvely while Referral and Email for marketing channels respectively 
for Ad Spend, the most funds were expended on Google Ads,Influencer marketing had the least funds, More funds were committed to Household items and the least to Bveragesn  for categories
The 5 top Bestsellers were Air Freshener, Moisturizer, Soap, Shampoo and Dishwasher and the 5 least  Selling Product were Coffee, Toothpaste, Energy Drinks, Protein bars and Nuts. 
Insights  
Given that the best sellers fall within the categories of household and Personal Care items, it is deduced that purchases are driven by necessity. 
Despite the cost of adverts and the total impression of 242M, only 1M was converted, this may imply wrong audience targeting, weak advertorials, unattractive pricing or poor user interface. The timeframe for exposure of impression was short term, this may also need to be reviewed.
Recommomendation:
 i. Need to explore other marketing channels including traditional marketing
ii. Campaigns need to be more strategic and audience specific 
iii. Pricing and user interphase should also be reviewed and made attractive 
iv. Check customer user interface to ensure it is user friendly
