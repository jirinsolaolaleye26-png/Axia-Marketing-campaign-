# Axia-Marketing-campaign-
 Microsoft Power BI was used to create  an interactive dashboard and determine the performance of a digital marketing campaign carried out from November 2024 to February 2025 for a company based in India across five(5) channels; Google ads, Email Campaigns, Instagram ads, Influencer marketing and Referrals for categories of products ranging from household items, benverages, Groceries, Personal Care to Snacks. A total of Two Hundred and Forty -Two Million (242M) Impressions were launched attracting Twelve Million (12M) clicks and One Million (IM) conversions. through One Thousand (1000) campaigns 

Insights

Highest Revenue by product was Cold drinks and lowest Energy drink, Highest Revenue by category was Household, Google Ads generated the most revenue and Emails the lowest. Household items had and Beverage had the highest and the highest ROI and the Email the least. Air freshener and Cofee had the highest and Lowest ROI respectively for products, while Referral and Email for marketing channels respectively 
The most funds were expended on Google Ads, Influencer marketing had the least funds on Marrketing channels , Household items had the highest and Beverages the least expended on the marketing channels for categories. Household and Personal Care products had  were the bessellers by category which indicates that purchases are driven by needs and not wants. 
Despite the cost of adverts and the total impression of 242M, only 1M was converted, this may imply wrong audience targeting, weak advertorials, unattractive pricing or poor user interface. The timeframe for exposure of impression was short term, this may also need to be reviewed.
