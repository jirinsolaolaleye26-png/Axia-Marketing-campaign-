# Axia-Marketing-campaign-
The project used  Microsoft Power BI to create  an interactive dashboard and investigates the performance of a digital marketing campaign  carried out from November 2024 to February 2025 for a company based in India across five(5) channels; Google ads, Email Campaigns, Instagram ads, Influencer marketing and Referrals for categories of products ranging from household items, benverages, Groceries, Personal Care to Snacks
A total of Two Hundred and Forty -Two Million (242M) Impressions were launched attracting Twelve Million (12M) clicks and One Million (IM) conversions. through One Thousand (1000) campaigns  
Insights

Highest Revenue by product was Cold drinks and lowest Energy drink, Highest Revenue by category was Household and the least was Personal Care, Google Ads generated the most revenue and Emails the lowest. Household items and Beverage had the highest and lowest Return on Investments, Air freshener and Cofee for products respectvely while Referral and Email for marketing channels respectively 
The most funds were expended on Google Ads,Influencer marketing had the least funds, More funds were committed to Household items and the least to Bveragesn  for categories.Best sellers are household and Personal Care products which  deduces that purchases are driven by necessity. 
Despite the cost of adverts and the total impression of 242M, only 1M was converted, this may imply wrong audience targeting, weak advertorials, unattractive pricing or poor user interface. The timeframe for exposure of impression was short term, this may also need to be reviewed.
